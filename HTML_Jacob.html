About me

I'm Luke Berliner and I'm a Concept Artist

I am currently the Lead Environment Concept Artist at Santa Monica Studio. I am passionate about art direction, world building, learning, and team building. My credits include God of War 2018, God of War Ascension, Transformers Prime, TV and Print Advertising, Lecture at my Alma mater Otis College of Art and Design where I taught classes in entertainment design for 2 years, as well as 4 years of teaching experience at Red Engine a small design school formerly located in Los Angeles. I am currently working on a new project with Santa Monica Studios which is in early development.
